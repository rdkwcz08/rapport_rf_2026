\begin{abstract}
This document presents the various steps carried out during the "RF Energy Harvesting" module taught by Mohsen Koohestani at ESEO. 
It covers the full progression of the project, from the initial system simulations performed using Analog Device Systems software, 
through to the study and design of a radio frequency energy harvesting system (RF Harvesting). 
The goal is to capture ambient electromagnetic energy from Wi-Fi and cellular networks in order to power low-consumption IoT sensors, 
with a particular focus on optimizing the impedance matching circuit and the rectenna (rectifying antenna).
\end{abstract}
